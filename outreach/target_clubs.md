## Approach Strategy

**Channel priority:**
1. Instagram DM to the coach's personal account (lower barrier than email for Turkish clubs — check who posts training content)
2. Club email (from website contact page)
3. LinkedIn (performance director or club president)

**Personalisation rule:** Reference one specific athlete or result from their recent competition before pitching. Check the federation results pages (tkf.org.tr, triatlonfed.org.tr, tbf.gov.tr) for recent race results.

**Opening offer:** "5 sporcunuzu 30 gün ücretsiz sisteme alalım — kredi kartı gerekmez."

**Sequence:**
- Day 0: First contact (email_01 or Instagram DM)
- Day 5: Follow-up if no reply (email_02 with sport-specific hook)
- Day 12: Single-line final check ("Zaman mı olmadı, yoksa ilginizi çekmedi mi?")
- Day 13+: Remove from active list, add to 90-day re-contact queue

**Federasyon approach (TKF, Triatlon Fed, TBF):**
- Lead with partnership framing, not sales: "Üye kulüplere ücretsiz erişim sunmak istiyoruz"
- Ask for a 30-minute call with the technical director, not the president
- Offer a co-branded pilot with 3 member clubs before any formal agreement
