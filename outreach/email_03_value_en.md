## Usage Notes

**Target audience:** UK, Irish, Australian, or international English-speaking clubs. Use this template when the contact is a head coach or performance director at a mid-to-large endurance club.

**Personalisation slots:**
- **[Name]:** First name of the coach or director
- Add one sentence referencing their club or a recent result before the opening line if you can find it

**Competitor positioning:**
- TrainingPeaks: expensive per-athlete pricing, US-centric support
- Final Surge: simpler feature set, no AI digest, no DFA-α1
- Garmin Connect / Strava: consumer tools, not coaching platforms

**Pricing note:** The £25/month figure is the international equivalent of ₺1000. Adjust to local currency (€30 for EU clubs, $30 USD for North American). Do not quote pricing in the subject line.

**Follow-up:** If no reply in 5 business days, send a single-line follow-up:
> "Hi [Name] — did this land in spam? Happy to share a 2-minute video instead."

**GDPR angle is the hook for European clubs** — lead with it in the opening if the club is in the EU or UK.
