## Retention

- **Training and health data:** 3 years from last activity
- **Export archives:** Signed URL valid 7 days; storage file purged after 30 days
- **Deletion requests:** 30-day grace period; permanent purge runs after
- **Audit logs:** 1 year (legal traceability, anonymised after account deletion)

## Your Rights (GDPR Article 15–22)

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

- **Access / portability (Art.20):** App → Profile → Data & Privacy → "Export my data". Downloads all your data as a JSON file. Link valid 7 days.
- **Erasure (Art.17):** App → Profile → Data & Privacy → "Delete my account". A 30-day grace period applies; you can cancel before the deadline. After the grace period, deletion is irreversible.
- **Rectification:** Update your profile data directly in the app.
- **Restriction / objection:** Manage consent granularly in Profile → Privacy Dashboard.
- **Withdraw consent:** Profile → Privacy Dashboard → "Withdraw consent".

For requests not covered by the in-app controls, email info@sporeus.com with subject line "GDPR Data Request".
